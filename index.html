Clean Water for All: The Nanomaterial Revolution

Current water purification methods struggle with efficiency and accessibility. Our project leverages cutting-edge nanomaterials to create affordable, scalable solutions that address multiple contaminants simultaneously.

Key Features

  • ⚡ Removes heavy metals, drugs, and pathogens
  • 🌍 Works with minimal infrastructure
  • 🔬 Multifunctional nanoparticle design

Why Existing Methods Fall Short

Reverse Osmosis

  • Wastes 3-4L per 1L purified
  • High energy consumption

Activated Carbon

  • Adsorbs organic compounds
  • Ineffective against heavy metals
  • Requires frequent replacement

Chlorination

  • Forms harmful DBPs
  • Limited residual effect
  • Corrosive to infrastructure

The Urgency

With 2 billion people lacking safe drinking water and microplastics found in 94% of US tap water, innovative solutions can't wait.

Portability

Benefits & Challenges

Global Impact: Scalable solution for water scarcity, especially in rural and disaster areas.
Sustainability: Energy-efficient, greener alternative to reverse osmosis and distillation.
Cost-Effective: Reduces reliance on expensive technologies, making clean water more accessible.
Health & Safety: Safe removal of nanoparticles from water is crucial to avoid toxicity.
Environmental Impact: Proper nanoparticle disposal is essential to avoid ecosystem contamination.
Scalability: Initial high costs require ongoing innovation and investment.
